Otzi The Iceman

The neolithic man found frozen around 5,000 years later on the glacier in the Schnalstal/Val Senales Valley.

Otzi's body as found in 1991 by two German tourists.

Otzi Fun Facts:

  • Otzi's body was found September 19th, 1991 in the Oztal Alps on the Austrian-Italian border.
  • He was found with some items typically found within the copper age. The items found were discovered to be, a copper axe with a yew handle, a flint bladed knife along with a quiver with arrows of dogwood shafts.
  • His death is believed to be by loss of blood, he was found with an aroowhead lodged in his left shoulder and a tramatic blow to the head.
